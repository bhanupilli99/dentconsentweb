Clinical AI Assistant
Is a root canal painful?
Modern root canals use local anesthesia, so you'll feel minimal discomfort. Most patients compare it to getting a completely standard filling.
How long does the procedure typically take?
A typical root canal takes about 60–90 minutes. Complex cases may require a second brief visit to ensure everything is perfect.
What are the risks?
Risks are minimal but can include minor infection, incomplete cleaning, or a rare tooth fracture. Your dentist will discuss these with you before proceeding.
